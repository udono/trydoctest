Access
------
The manipulation of records in this model is controlled by
:ref:`ir_model_access` entries.
:ref:`res_user`\ s being member of :ref:`res_group` "Timesheet Administration"
have the following access permissions:

    :Read: allowed
    :Write: allowed
    :Create: allowed
    :Delete: allowed


All other :ref:`res_user`\ s have these access permissions:

    :Read: allowed
    :Write: forbidden
    :Create: forbidden
    :Delete: forbidden
